ABOUT LAGOS TRANSPORT FEST

Nigeria’s Transport industry is the gateway to the economy of the nation and backbone that facilitates trade, supply chain and economic success in the most populous African nation, In the past two years, Lagos Transport Fest has facilitated key discussions and networking amongst the country’s public and private sector stakeholders within the industry, It is worthy to note that, the Country’s ticketing platform for the Railway industry was showcased and launched at the 2019 Lagos Transport Fest.

With a view to consolidating on previous gains, Lagos Transport Fest is back for its 3rd year, This year’s edition of the event has been broadly expanded to play host to key public and private sector stakeholders across Road, Maritime, Logistics and Rail industries.

Over 2 days, Lagos Transport Fest will lead high calibre public and private sector deliberations aimed at shaping the future of Nigeria’s Transport Sector. The event will combine conferences & exhibitions providing mobility companies with an opportunity to showcase their brand offerings to industry leaders at the event.

Lagos Transport Fest will provide mobility stakeholders with an opportunity to drive discussions across Transport Infrastructure, Investments, Policies and Innovations, The event will contribute key deliberations and recommendations to the National Transport Policy currently driven by the Federal Republic of Nigeria. At the fore of the event is networking- facilitating deals and engagements between industry leaders across the transport industry.

The event is annually organized by Eventhive.ng, Nigeria’s leading event servicing company with events portfolios across the country’s Power, Oil & Gas, Construction, Agribusiness, Real Estate, Transport and Tech industries.
