- Insights

2025 Key Content Tracks

Payments & Remittances

This track will explore the evolving landscape of domestic and cross-border payments in Nigeria, highlighting innovations in real-time transfers, mobile money, diaspora remittances, and the role of fintechs in driving financial inclusion.

Regulation

This track will examine the regulatory ecosystem shaping Nigeria’s fintech sector, focusing on policy reforms, sandbox frameworks, oversight by the CBN and SEC, and the balance between innovation and consumer protection.

Crypto & DeFi

This track will unpack the state of cryptocurrencies and decentralized finance (DeFi) in Nigeria, covering peer-to-peer trading, blockchain adoption, regulatory responses, and the opportunities and risks within the DeFi ecosystem.

Lending & Credit

This track will analyze the digital lending space in Nigeria, including credit scoring innovations, BNPL models, micro-lending, regulatory compliance, and the challenges of responsible lending and recovery.

AI, Fraud & Security

This track will investigate the role of artificial intelligence in enhancing fraud detection, cybersecurity, and risk mitigation in Nigeria’s fintech sector, while addressing the ethical and regulatory dimensions of AI use.

Embedded Finance

This track will explore how embedded finance is transforming user experiences by integrating financial services within non-financial platforms, including trends in APIs, partnerships, and financial product distribution.

Wealth, Investment & Asset Management

This track will highlight digital solutions for savings, wealth management, and investing in Nigeria, including robo-advisors, crypto investments, and strategies for democratizing access to financial markets.

Agent Banking

This track will delve into the critical role of agent networks in expanding financial services to underserved populations in Nigeria, examining operational models, technology enablement, and regulatory support.

Customer Experience

This track will focus on how fintechs in Nigeria are leveraging UX/UI design, data personalization, and omnichannel strategies to enhance customer engagement, retention, and satisfaction across financial products.
