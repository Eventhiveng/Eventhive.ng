AFRICA FIRST- THE PRESENT FUTURE OF TECH

LAGOS TECH FEST,

is a high calibre tech focused conference. The event annually provides a veritable platform for discussions and deal making between leading private and public sector tech stakeholders. The conference focuses on addressing key obstacles in the West African tech ecosystem while uncovering the countless opportunities within the Nigerian and West African tech market.

Lagos Tech Fest brings together entrepreneurs, disruptors, innovators, investors, venture capitalists, tech enthusiasts with a view to exploring opportunities within the tech ecosystem. Lagos Tech Fest harmonizes stakeholders in the tech industry for learning, knowledge transfer and peer-to-peer networking. Keynote and specialist session content will be unique to the conference as it is the result of public and private sector collaboration.

Lagos Tech Fest puts strategy, policy and thought leadership at the heart of its sessions ensuring a delegate audience of key decision makers and c-suite directors.

Join 500+ delegates attendees to network, learn, transact and discuss opportunities and issues within Nigeria's Tech ecosystem
