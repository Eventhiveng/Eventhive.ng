About Us

Lagos Real Estate Fest is an annual gathering of the most influential and knowledgeable public and private sector stakeholders, within Nigeria's property ecosystem. The event now in its 2nd year is West Africa’s leading marketplace for property stakeholders combining conference and exhibitions.

Lagos Real Estate Fest is a property-focused forum that harmonizes stakeholders in the property industry for learning, knowledge transfer and peer-to-peer networking. Keynote and specialist session content will be unique to the conference as it is the result of public and private sector collaboration.

Lagos Real Estate Fest puts strategy, policy and thought leadership at the heart of its sessions ensuring a delegate audience of key decision makers and c-suite directors.

Lagos Real Estate Fest is annually organized by Eventhive.ng, West Africa’s leading PR Firm with core competencies in developing key Industries via B2B Events and Exhibitions.

Join 500+ delegates from real estate, design, finance, developers,construction architecture and other property players to network, learn, transact and discuss opportunities and issues within Nigeria's real estate ecosystem.


WHY YOU SHOULD ATTEND

Lagos Real Estate Fest annually creates opportunity to:

  • - Provide a global platform for debate and action to build Nigeria's real estate industry.

  • - Explore business opportunities within the real estate industry

  • - Learn on how Real Estate is evolving with the rise of innovation.

  • - Provide a meeting point for buyers and sellers in the Real estate Industry

  • - Share conversations with Policy makers as regards what they are doing to support innovation and investment.

  • - Network with policy-setting government figures at Federal and State Government levels and benefit from collaboration and knowledge transfer.

WHAT'S NEW IN 2021?

This year’s edition of Lagos Real Estate Fest will refocus conversations on solutions rather than challenges, analysing market conditions as the industry gradually recovers from the pandemic, With keen interest on the short and long term solutions. This year’s edition of the event will spotlight discussions around de-risking, attracting and retaining investments in the Real estate sector, Navigating the changing model within the real estate market.

The event will combine conference and exhibition providing the public with an opportunity to attend and discuss with property developers and real estate companies on available property offerings.
