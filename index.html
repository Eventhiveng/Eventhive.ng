WHY RWANDA?

Rwanda is rapidly growing as a well-known tech hub in Africa, creating the kind of tech ecosystem that signals and attracts the attention of investors and startups alike. Rwanda’s ICT policies and solutions have helped the government to lay a solid foundation that are aiding growth of various sectors, including transport, governance, education, trade and agriculture. This year’s Africa Tech Forum will be held in the beautiful city of Kigali, as we work to bring together African tech leaders for this important gathering.

WE LOOK FORWARD TO HOSTING YOU IN RWANDA.

BRINGING TOGETHER AFRICA’S LEADING INDUSTRY EXPERTS, INVESTORS,STAKEHOLDERS AND POLICY MAKERS ACROSS THE TECH ECOSYSTEM

Eventhive.ng is delighted to announce the flagship edition of the Africa Tech Forum scheduled to hold on the 31st of May, 2023 at The Kigali Convention Center, Kigali, Rwanda.

As technology continues to grow across Africa, It has become important to continue to connect, engage and dialogue with stakeholders across the industry, that is what Africa Tech Forum has been designed to achieve, in Rwanda. Africa Tech Forum will provide an opportunity to showcase technology solutions for businesses, connect technology providers with new markets and provide access-to-market opportunities for tech startups.

Africa Tech Forum will unite the entirety of Africa’s tech market, highlighting the important role the industry plays, exploring available opportunities in the market, discussing some of the challenges facing the industry whilst defining the future of the ecosystem. The event will deliver an unrivaled networking and deal-making opportunities, as we work to further the development of Africa’s tech markets.

Africa Tech Forum will combine conferences & exhibitions, beyond the conversations and networking, the event will provide a platform for companies working in the tech value chain, to showcase their solutions, through available exhibition opportunities at the event.

Africa Tech Forum is proudly organized by Eventhive.ng, Africa’s leading B2B event servicing company with event portfolio’s across Energy, Tech, Real Estate and Transport.
