ABOUT LREF 2023 - 4TH EDITION

Eventhive.ng is delighted to announce that Lagos Real Estate Fest will return on the 26TH of June, 2023.

Now in its 4th year, Lagos Real Estate Fest is a high caliber Nigeria property-focused conference, exhibitions and networking platform. The event annually provides a veritable platform that convenes property stakeholders, investors, developers, policy makers, amongst other sector leaders working across the entirety of the property value chain. The event annually brings together these stakeholders, providing them with an opportunity to define the future of the property industry, debate and discuss key developments in the property ecosystem, share knowledge and network.

As Nigeria prepares for a new country-wide leadership, the event will set the agenda for workable strategies for meeting the increasing housing deficit. With investments at its core, this year’s edition of the event will be headlined by building a platform that focuses on connecting international investments into our local opportunities.

Lagos Real Estate Fest will combine conferences and exhibitions. Beyond the conversations and networking, the event will provide a platform for companies working in the property value chain, to showcase their solutions and product offerings, through available exhibition opportunities at the event.

Lagos Real Estate Fest is annually organized by Eventhive.ng, Nigeria’s leading B2B2C event servicing company with event portfolios across Oil & Gas, Power, Tech, Real Estate and Transport in Nigeria, UK, US and Rwanda.
