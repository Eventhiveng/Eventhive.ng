Unlocking opportunities and innovations in Africa's Emerging Insurance Market

With a population of 211 million and insurance penetration below 1%, Nigeria is a key player in Africa’s insurance market. Despite its potential, growth is hindered by consumer mistrust, low awareness, limited purchasing power, and outdated processes. Addressing these barriers is essential to unlocking opportunities.

Innovative insurtech solutions are paving the way by simplifying claims, improving premium collection, and expanding last-mile access. These advancements aim to enhance customer acquisition and build trust, positioning Nigeria’s insurance market for transformation.

The Nigeria Insurance Forum brings together industry leaders, regulators, and stakeholders to discuss challenges and opportunities in this evolving landscape. The event will explore strategies for sustainable growth, offering valuable insights to shape the future of insurance in Nigeria.

Nigeria Insurance Forum - Agenda

Redefining Insurance: Innovation and Disruption in the Nigerian Market

This panel session will explore the power of innovation and technology in the Nigerian insurance industry. As the industry evolves, new business models, digital solutions, and innovative products are emerging to address the unique needs of the Nigerian market.

Key discussion areas will include:

  • Digital transformation: Exploring the impact of digital technologies on insurance operations, customer experience, and product delivery.
  • Insurtech and Innovation: Enhancing customer engagement and satisfaction through personalized products and seamless digital experiences.
  • Product Innovation: Developing innovative insurance products tailored to the specific needs of the Nigerian market.
  • Regulatory Landscape: Navigating the evolving regulatory environment and advocating for policies that support industry growth.
  • Financial Inclusion: Expanding access to insurance products and services, particularly for underserved segments of the population.
  • Economic realities: Examining the impact of macroeconomic conditions such as inflation on the insurance industry.

How Insurtech Startups are Reshaping Nigeria’s Insurance Industry

This panel session will explore the impact of insurtech startups on Nigeria's insurance industry. Panelists will discuss how these innovative companies are leveraging technology to disrupt traditional insurance models, improve customer experience, and expand access to insurance products.

Key discussion areas will include:

  • Product Innovation: The development of innovative insurance products tailored to the needs of the Nigerian market.
  • Customer Experience: Improving customer experience through digital channels, personalized services, and real-time claims processing.
  • Partnership and Collaboration: Exploring opportunities for collaboration between traditional insurers and insurtech startups.
  • Regulatory Landscape: Examining the role of the government and regulators in shaping policies that support innovation in the insurtech market.
  • Funding and Investment: How NIgerian insurtech startups can secure funding and attract investment to fuel growth and scale.

Microinsurance in Nigeria: Assessing the Past, Shaping the Future

This panel session will delve into the evolution of microinsurance in Nigeria over the past decade. Panelists will discuss the key challenges and opportunities facing the microinsurance industry, as well as the impact of recent regulatory developments and technological advancements.

Key discussion areas will include:

  • Regulatory Landscape: Examining the role of regulators in shaping the microinsurance industry and addressing emerging challenges over the years.
  • Product Innovation: Exploring innovative microinsurance products and use cases tailored to the needs of underserved populations.
  • Distribution Channels: Analyzing the most effective channels for delivering microinsurance products, including agent networks, mobile platforms, and partnerships with other financial institutions.
  • Consumer Education and Awareness: The importance of educating consumers about the benefits of microinsurance and addressing misconceptions.
  • Financial Inclusion: The role of microinsurance in promoting financial inclusion and reducing poverty.
  • Risk Management and Sustainability: Addressing the challenges of risk assessment, pricing, and claims management in the microinsurance context.
