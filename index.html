WHAT'S NEW IN 2024

More Stages

To expand the focus of discussions at the Nigeria Oil & Gas, we are delighted to introduce a new stage, beyond the main stage, providing you with an opportunity to choose the sessions that interest you.

Pre-event Networking Drinks

To aid 1-2-1 networking, we are introducing a pre-event networking drinks for executives and government representatives that will be participating at the event, providing an opportunity to network in a relaxed setting.

Leaders Forum

This year's line-up of speakers will target C-level innovative company leaders, who are redefining the future of Nigeria's Oil & Gas Industry.

Expo Hall

At this year's edition of the Nigeria Oil & Gas. We are introducing an expo hall, providing Oil & Gas companies with an opportunity to showcase their solutions/products to delegates at the event.
