At the forefront of East Africa's innovation

East Africa's tech ecosystem is rapidly growing, the region is experiencing a surge in technology adoption and innovation, fueled by a young and tech-savvy population, and boasts a vibrant startup culture with numerous incubators and accelerators. Key sectors like fintech, agritech, and climate tech are driving this growth, East Africa Tech Forum, previously known as Africa Tech Forum, returns for its 3rd edition, to exclusively focus on the growth opportunities across East Africa's tech ecosystem. East Africa Tech Forum provides a veritable meeting place for startups, regulators, investors, enthusiasts, disruptors and everyone working across various layers of the ecosystem
