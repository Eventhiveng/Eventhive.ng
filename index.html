About Us

Lagos Power Fest is the foremost gathering of the industry's finest minds, top-level business leaders and decision-makers within Nigeria's Power and Energy sector. At the conference & exhibition, industry experts will share major insights concerning the Nigeria's Power market.

Lagos Power Fest will provide an opportunity for learning, knowledge transfer, interaction and peer-to-peer networking. Keynote and specialist session content will be unique to the conference as it is the result of public and private sector collaboration.

Lagos Power Fest puts strategy, policy and thought leadership at the heart of its sessions ensuring a delegate audience of key decision-makers and c-suite directors.

Join 1000+ delegates and thought leaders, trend setters to refresh thinking, exchange ideas on the future of Power and connect with other stakeholders & decision makers.


Why you should attend

Lagos Power Fest will create an opportunity to:

  • - Provide a global platform for debate and action to build the Nigeria's Power sector.

  • - Explore business opportunities within the Power and Energy sector.

  • - Learn on how Power is evolving with the rise of digitisation.

  • - Share conversations with policy makers as regards what they are doing to support innovation and investment in the Power sector.

  • - Network with policy-setting government figures at Federal and State Governments levels and benefit from collaboration and knowledge transfer.

  • - Network with Industry Leaders in the Power and Energy sector.

Who you will meet at the event

  • Federal and State Governments and their agencies.

  • Power sector subject matter experts.

  • Oil and Gas Companies.

  • Equipment Vendors

  • Grid Operators

  • Power Utilities

  • Off-grid Energy Suppliers

  • International Development Partners

  • Private Equity and Investment Banks

  • Energy Sector Heads at Financial Institutions.
