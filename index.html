Download Post Event Report


ABOUT

As Nigeria works to totally overhaul its Oil & Gas Industry via the passing of the Petroleum Industry Bill and a renewed commitment to natural gas from the leadership, We are excited to announce our flagship Oil & Gas event tagged, Nigeria Oil & Gas Outlook, The event united thought leaders, operators, regulators and stakeholders in the Oil & Gas value chain.

With a renewed focus of convening key players in Nigeria’s energy ecosystem with a view to exploring, debating and discussing regulatory issues facing operators in the oil & gas sector and policy makers.

The event provided an unrivalled platform to also discuss the marginal fields licensing round held in 2020, bringing new players into the industry, As the industry grapple with fall out from COVID-19 which has led to low oil prices and economic challenges, Attracting Investment was at the core of conversation at this edition of the event.

Nigeria Oil & Gas Outlook played host to key government officials together with industry stakeholders at the one-day event of key learnings, networking and deal-making. NOGO 2021 puts strategy, policy and thought leadership at the heart of its session ensuring a delegate audience of key decision makers and c-suite directors.

The event is annually organized by Eventhive.ng, West Africa’s leading PR Firm with core competencies in developing key industries via B2B Events and exhibitions.
