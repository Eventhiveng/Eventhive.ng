LAGOS TRANSPORT FEST,

now in its 2nd year is West Africa's leading high calibre gathering of transport stakeholders spanning across Road, Rail, Logistics and Port sectors , to share ideas, innovate and make meaningful connections.

Lagos Transport Fest will provide an opportunity for learning, knowledge transfer, interaction and peer-to-peer networking. Keynote and specialist session content will be unique to the conference as it is the result of public and private sector collaboration.

Lagos Transport Fest puts strategy, policy and thought leadership at the heart of its sessions, encouraging a delegate audience of key decision makers and C-suite directors.

Lagos Transport is organized, annually, by Eventhive.ng West Africa's leading PR Firm with core competencies in developing Africa's businesses via face-to-face/virtual events.
