ABOUT

The UK-Nigeria Business Outlook is expected to bring together Nigerian leaders, international business chief executives and heads of international organizations to create new partnerships that will deliver investments into Nigeria. The event will provide Nigeria with a forum to project itself as a leading investment destination in Africa. The event will provide Nigerian representatives with an opportunity to highlight policy and legislation efforts towards improving investment and business climate in the country.

UK-Nigeria Business Outlook will explore new perspectives on UK-Nigeria partnership for mutual growth and development. Trade, Energy, Tech and Real Estate are expected to dominate discussions at the event scheduled to be held between 30-31 March, 2022 at the One BirdCage Walk Event Center, Westminster, London, SW1H99J.

UK-Nigeria Business Outlook will provide a platform for public-private dialogue, delivering high level meetings of CEO’s and investors with Nigerian Government Officials, to gain insight into the country’s economic development strategies. The sessions will provide exposure to a variety of key sectors, business environments and public/private investment opportunities. The event will be a hybrid event, combining an in-person conference in London and an opportunity for virtual participation. The event will offer an opportunity for fantastic networking providing industry leaders with an opportunity to meet and do business.

The event is annually organized by Eventhive.ng, West Africa’s leading B2B event servicing company with event portfolios across Oil & Gas, Power, Transport, Real Estate, Construction, Agribusiness and Tech.
