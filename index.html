Shaping the Future of Nigeria's Transport Industry

In its 6th year, the Lagos Transport Fest brings together industry experts from road mobility, rail, maritime, logistics, and supply chain sectors to influence and define the future of Nigeria's transport industry. Through conferences, exhibitions, and networking opportunities, the event serves as a pivotal platform for fostering collaboration among stakeholders, investors, and government regulators in the transport sector.

- What's new in 2024

Exciting New Features to Enhance Your Lagos Transport Fest Experience

The Lagos Transport Fest introduces fresh elements to foster deeper connections and showcase industry advancements. This year's event features an expanded expo area, a regulatory forum for direct dialogue with industry regulators, and a premier pre-event networking mixer. Attendees will also benefit from a dedicated networking lounge for focused interactions, and the introduction of industry awards to honor leaders driving progress in Nigeria's transport sector.

Expo Area

We are expanding the expo area at this year’s edition of the Lagos Transport Fest providing various players with an opportunity to showcase their services/solution

Regulatory Forum

We are introducing a regulatory forum at this year’s edition of the event, to ensure one-to-one engagement between industry players and regulators across various verticals of the transport industry.

Pre-Event Networking Mixer

We are introducing the flagship pre-event networking mixer for players across maritime, mobility, rail, logistics and supply chain verticals of the transport industry, providing them with an opportunity to network in a relaxed setting.

Networking Lounge

We are developing quick 1-2-1 designated networking lounge at this year’s edition of the event, providing industry players with an opportunity to engage in a secluded environment.

Industry Award

At this year’s edition of the event, we will be rewarding industry players that have demonstrated commitment to Nigeria’s transport market.
