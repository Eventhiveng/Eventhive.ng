ABOUT

The Nigeria Oil & Gas Outlook Conference & Exhibition is returning to Lagos for its second edition. Building on the successes recorded from the flagship edition of the event in 2021 which was the first energy event since the PIB became an Act, The event has grown to become a very important meeting place for Nigeria’s Oil & Gas stakeholders. The event is scheduled to be held on the 31st of August, 2022 at the Civic Center, Victoria Island, Lagos, Nigeria.

Against the backdrop from the pandemic, The need to demonstrate longstanding commitment to investment in Nigeria’s energy industry has become extremely important, NOGO 2022 will provide a premier platform to debate, discuss and examine very important challenges as the country works to close the gap of decline in production, oil theft, expand and promote exploration, reach fuel-independency via large scale investments in refining capacity, diversify her economy and ensure a just energy transition by developing its natural gas resources and other alternative energy resources.

Attendees at this year’s edition of the event will benefit from a wide range of industry-advancing panel sessions, in-conversations, keynote sessions, presentations, unrivaled networking, investor/government/private sector deal rooms, as the event works to promote opportunities in Nigeria’s Oil & Gas Industry. The event will tackle topics spanning across Local content; Energy Transition; Gas Market; Petroleum Industry Act; Women in Energy amongst other latest developments aimed at strengthening the development of Africa’s largest producer of oil - Nigeria.

The event is annually organized by Eventhive.ng, West Africa’s leading PR Firm with core competencies in developing key industries via B2B Events and exhibitions.
