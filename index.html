Returning for its 5th edition, Lagos Transport Fest plays host to the most senior leaders across Nigeria’s transport and logistics, uniting industry stakeholders who are defining the future of the ecosystem. If your work resonates with road, rail, maritime, or logistics, this is where you should be.

The event is annually organized by Eventhive.ng, West Africa’s leading International Trade/B2B event servicing company with event portfolios across Energy, Tech, Real Estate and Transport in Nigeria, UK, US and Rwanda.

TESTIMONIALS

"I was on a panel session at the 4th edition of the Lagos Transport Fest held at the Civic Center yesterday.

The panelists spoke brilliantly on the theme of discussion "NIGERIA'S MULTIMODAL INTEGRATED TRANSPORT SOLUTION; UNPACKING ROAD, RAIL AND WATER OPTIONS""

Sola Giwa

Specus Adviser on Transportation to The Lagos State Governor

"Lagos Transport Fest ensured that all the stakeholders within the Transport Ecosystem were present for the conversation.

Transportation and Logistics industry is facing an era of unprecedented change with digitization and customer behavior and expectations evolve. New technologies are enabling greater efficiency and more collaborative operating models; they're also re-shaping the marketplace in ways that are only just beginning to become apparent. New entrants, whether they be start-ups or the industry's own customers and suppliers, are also shaking up the sector with improved technology. There is a paradigm shift in consumer behavior and thinking.

Interswitch Group presented during the fireside, where we introduced the newly launched Quickteller Transport Solution that avails consumers opportunity to book and purchase tickets from numerous transport operators at their convenience. It allows for fare comparison amongst the operators across all modes of transport."

Nnenna Ajanwachuku

Business Development Manager - Mass Transit, Indeco, Interswitch Group

"Were you at the Lagos Transport Fest yesterday?

What was your favorite session?

We had our Senior Manager B2B; Seun Ariori speak in a panel session that focused on Transport Tech & Investments: Exploring Trends Shaping Africa's Mobility Market.

It was an insightful session where we had Seun Ariori speak on how the rise in import duties in Nigeria and fluctuation in exchange rates affect aspiring vehicle owners and the general vehicle asset financing market.

It was indeed an insightful session. Thank you Lagos Transport Fest for having us."
