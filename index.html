FINTECH WITHOUT BORDERS FORUM

Hogan Lovells, Atlantic House, London, United Kingdom | 25th October, 2024

Leading gathering of players across various verticals of the borderless fintech ecosystem.

GET A TICKET REQUEST BROCHURE

Fintech Without Borders Forum is dedicated to showcasing the important role of borderless fintech in the global fintech ecosystem.

The event will focus on important issues facing global cross-border payments landscape-best approaches in achieving faster, safer, more transparent cross-boder payments, global fintech infrastructure, FX liquidity challenges, Impact of fraud and compliance on cross-border payments, evolving border financial services and fintech industry in Africa, amongst other issues.

The event will provide an unrivaled opportunity for companies in cross-border payments, B2B payments, banks, consulting, technology providers, governments representatives to network, discover new partnerships and discuss the most relevant topics across the borderless fintech industry. Industry leaders will gather to share insights and best practices on developing more efficient cross-boder payment systems to support global economic activity.

Our mission is targeted at fostering growth and innovation in the cross- border payments space and help companies stay ahead of the curve.

  • Convene a gathering of global senior-level attendees from international payments, banks and everyone working across various verticals of the borderless fintech industry.
  • The event will provide an opportunity for you to meet suppliers, in a comfortable environment that fosters collaboration and potential partnerships.
  • Access all the right industry solution providers to explore cutting-edge strategies, technologies, and challenges facing the borderless payments ecosystem.
  • Learn about and discuss current and future technologies and trends with industry colleagues.

WHAT CAN ATTENDEES EXPECT?

Attendees at the Fintech Without Borders Forum will;

  1. Gain better insights as to how the borderless fintech landscape has evolved and what the future holds
  2. Stay abreast with emerging technology trends that are shaping the borderless fintech landscape
  3. Explore the realities of Central Bank Digital Currencies (CBDS)
  4. Have a closer look at how to minimise fraud and mitigate cybercrime
  5. Find out how to overcome regulatory and compliance barriers
  6. Network with fellow delegates, speakers & exhibitors to improve your business contacts and relationships
