What's next for Nigeria's digital assets industry?

With SEC Licensing, crypto exchange companies in Nigeria, signaling the industry's role in Nigeria's digital finance future, stakeholders will converge at the Crypto & DEFI Forum, bringing together industry leaders across the digital assets industry, regulators, investors, innovators and users of digital assets solutions to explore emerging opportunities, and contribute to shaping the future of crypto and digital assets in Nigeria.

If your work resonates with Web3, Crypto and DeFi, this is where you should be.

2025 Key Content Tracks

Policy Regulation

This track will delve into the legal and regulatory frameworks governing crypto and digital assets in Nigeria, including licensing, compliance, taxation, and anti-money laundering(AML) measures.

Market Trends and Investment

This track will explore current market trends, investemnt strategies, and emerging opportunities in the Nigerian crypto and digital asset space. Topics may include DEFI's and stablecoins.

Blockchain & Web3 Innovations

This track will showcase the latest technological advancements in blockchain, distributed ledger technology (DLT), and other related areas. It will also explore the potential of these technologies to drive innovation in various sectors.

Financial Inclusion & Accessibility

This track will focus on how crypto and digital assets can promote financial inclusion and expand access to financial services for undeserved populations in Nigeria.

Security & Risk Management

This track will address the security risks associated with crypto and digital assets, including fraud, scams, and cyberattacks. It will also explore best practices for risk management and cybersecurity.

Education and Awareness

This track will focus on educating the public about crypto and digital assets, addressing common misconceptions and promoting responsible adoption.

Building the Ecosystem

This track will explore the development of the Nigerian crypto and digital asset ecosystem, including discussions on infrastructure, talent development, and community building.

Use Cases and Applications

This track will showcase real-world use cases of crypto and digital assets in various industries, such as finance, supply chain management, and healthcare.
