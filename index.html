This event will bring together key stakeholders—from venture capitalists t o f u n d managers—to explore investment opportunities, address market challenges, and chart a course for sustainable growth.

Key Focus Discussions

Building Sustainable Portfolios: Aligning Strategic Capital with Long-Term Growth in Nigeria

This panel will explore how investors can strategically deploy capital to build sustainable portfolios that drive long-term growth and create positive impact in Nigeria. It will delve into the critical aspects of aligning investment strategies with the country's development priorities and ensuring responsible capital allocation.

Decoding the Nigerian Investment Landscape: Opportunities and Risks

This panel will provide an in-depth analysis of the current investment climate in Nigeria, highlighting key opportunities and potential risks.

Focus discussion areas include:

  • Macroeconomic trends and their impact on investment.
  • Identifying high-growth sectors and emerging opportunities.
  • Navigating political and regulatory uncertainties.
  • Assessing and mitigating currency and market risks.
  • The impact of rising inflation on investment returns.

The future of PE/VC in Nigeria: Regulatory Reforms and Market Development

This panel will discuss the evolving regulatory landscape and its impact on PE/VC investments, exploring strategies for promoting market development and attracting capital
