Fintech Without Borders Forum 2025

Fintech Without Borders Forum is dedicated to showcasing the important role of borderless fintech in the global fintech ecosystem. The event annually focuses on important issues facing global cross-border payments landscape-best approaches in achieving faster, safer, more transparent cross-boder payments.

October 23rd | Convene, London.

Where borderless fintech leaders converge

Connecting stakeholders across the borderless fintech ecosystem

The event will provide an unrivaled opportunity for companies in cross-border payments, B2B payments, banks, consulting, technology providers, governments representatives to network, discover new partnerships and discuss the most relevant topics across the borderless fintech industry.
