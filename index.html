AFRICA FIRST- THE PRESENT FUTURE OF TECH

LAGOS TECH FEST,

is a high calibre tech focused conference. The event annually provides a veritable platform for discussions and deal making between leading private and public sector tech stakeholders. The conference focuses on addressing key obstacles in the West African tech ecosystem while uncovering the countless opportunities within the Nigerian and West African tech market.

Lagos Tech Fest brings together entrepreneurs, disruptors, innovators, investors, venture capitalists, tech enthusiasts with a view to exploring opportunities within the tech ecosystem. Lagos Tech Fest harmonizes stakeholders in the tech industry for learning, knowledge transfer and peer-to-peer networking. Keynote and specialist session content will be unique to the conference as it is the result of public and private sector collaboration.

Lagos Tech Fest puts strategy, policy and thought leadership at the heart of its sessions ensuring a delegate audience of key decision makers and c-suite directors.

Join 500+ delegates attendees to network, learn, transact and discuss opportunities and issues within Nigeria's Tech ecosystem


OUR KEY DELIVERABLES

-VIRTUAL SESSION WITH TECH THOUGHT LEADERS Lagos Tech Fest is a 2-day high caliber gathering of tech stakeholders. Participate in a 2-day live sessions and presentations with industry peers delivered by thought leaders within the tech ecosystem.

VIRTUAL EXHIBITION BOOTHS Lagos Tech Fest provides an opportunity to engage with leading brands in our innovative virtual sponsored exhibition booth. Our virtual booths provides virtual access to some of Nigeria's leading tech products and services

VIRTUAL NETWORKING Lagos Tech Fest provides an opportunity to engage and make meaningful connections virtually. With optimum usage of video and chat box networking function, the event provides an innovative networking experience for delegates, speakers and organizations.

Lagos Tech Fest creates an opportunity to;

- Provide a global platform for debate and action to build Nigeria's tech ecosystem
- Explore business opportunities within the tech ecosystem
- Learn on how Investments is responding to predicted growth within the tech ecosystem
- Discuss regulatory frameworks across key components of the tech ecosystem
- Share conversations with Policy makers as regards what they are doing to support innovation and investment.
- Network with policy-setting government figures at Federal and State Government levels and benefit from collaboration and knowledge transfer.
