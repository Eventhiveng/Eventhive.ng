SNG Lead 2022 Nigeria Oil & Gas Outlook Conference Holds August 31st In Lagos

The 2022 edition of the Nigeria Oil & Gas Outlook, which is going to be hosted by Eventhive.ng company is billed to hold in Lagos on August 31st and is going to be lead by Shell Nigeria Gas (SNG), which operates a growing world class gas transmission and distribution network in Nigeria.

The Nigeria Oil & Gas Outlook, a one-day conference has been designed to gather public and private sector stakeholders across Nigeria’s Oil & Gas industry, which will feature keynote presentations, panel discussion, breakout sessions, exhibitions, and fireside chats.

The event, which is scheduled to hold at the Civic Centre, Victoria Island, Lagos on August 31, 2022 with the theme: “Energy as a Foundation for Nigeria’s shared Prosperity and Unity.”

The focus of the event is to provide an opportunity for participating stakeholders to explore essential topics across local content, PIA, gas market and Energy Transition. The deliberation is targeted at positioning Nigeria’s energy industry for further growth and development.

Speaking on the event that was launched last year, the CEO of Eventhive.ng, Jamiu Ijaodola said,

The event was designed to channel the interests of industry players within Nigeria’s energy ecosystem with keen focus on the oil & gas players.

The expected outcome of the conference and exhibition is to help industry stakeholders network, learn and define the future of the energy ecosystem towards implementing policies that promote growth in Nigeria and Africa at large.
